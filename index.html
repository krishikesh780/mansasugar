FAQ

Mansa Sugar was founded in 2017 with the mission of producing high-quality sugar and related products for both the Zambian and diaspora markets. The company is dedicated to maintaining values of integrity, innovation, and sustainability.

Mansa Sugar believes in community development and has undertaken various initiatives in partnership with local organizations. These initiatives encompass healthcare and education projects, which have led to the transformation of the local economy and the growth of Chembe into a thriving hub.

Mansa Sugar is a wholly owned subsidiary of Spin Ventures Group. The group was founded by Shamji Vaja Gada (SVG) in 1926, who journeyed from India to Africa and established his roots in Kenya. Over time, SVG's family expanded their business from textile trading to various sectors including agriculture, agro-processing manufacturing, real estate, and asset management across multiple countries.

In addition to Zambia, Spin Ventures Group operates in several other countries, including Kenya, Uganda, and possibly more. The group has diversified its business operations across different sectors and geographies since its establishment in 1926.
